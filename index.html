DTS Gas Guru

Privacy Policy

Black Water Tek  |  Effective Date: March 2026

1. Introduction

DTS Gas Guru is a technical diving gas analysis application developed by Black Water Tek. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles your information when you use our mobile app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

DTS Gas Guru does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Dive logs or dive history
  • Gas calculation inputs or results
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Payment information

2.2 Data That Stays On Your Device

All gas calculations performed within the app are processed entirely on your device. No calculation data is sent to any server. Any preferences or settings you configure remain stored locally on your device only.

3. How the App Works

DTS Gas Guru performs all calculations locally on your device without any internet connectivity requirement. The app does not require an account, login, or registration of any kind. Your diving data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

DTS Gas Guru does not integrate with any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers. We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information in the first place.

4.1 App Store Platforms

When you download DTS Gas Guru through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, those platforms may collect information about the download in accordance with their own privacy policies. This data is handled entirely by Apple or Google and is not accessible to Black Water Tek.

5. Children's Privacy

DTS Gas Guru is intended for use by qualified technical divers and dive professionals. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Data Security

Since DTS Gas Guru does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being exposed through our application. Your device's own security measures protect any locally stored app preferences.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

We recommend checking this policy periodically for any updates.
